Building with AI

Press B and type what you want — like "a cozy cottage," "a stone castle," or "a rainbow." The AI builds it in front of you, blocks and all. You can also edit existing builds by describing changes.

AI Build Models

In Settings (the gear icon) you can choose between two AI models. Here's the difference, same prompt — "a cozy cottage":

Faster (default)

Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite — quick, simpler builds, lower cost.

Better Quality

Gemini 3.5 Flash — slower, more detailed and coherent builds.

Tip: There's a limit of 10 AI builds per day. The Faster model is the default to keep things quick — switch to Better Quality in Settings when you want more detailed builds.
